a bit more about me...

Presently, I am the senior manager of experience design for commercial and business systems at Takeda Oncology. I spend my days leading our UX initiatives, coaching the development teams through product builds and partnering with my customers to ensure our solutions meet their needs. Prior to my current position, I spent several years working in market research, analytics and data visualization.
