Motley Interactive Magazine

client: motley magazine, llc  format: digital size: forced portrait orientation (1536 x 2048 and 768 x 1024)

Motley Magazine features an eclectic assortment of articles covering topics such as entertainment, music, art, photography, emerging trends, environment, lifestyle, and occasionally politics. Although the magazine is published in both print and digital media, Motley’s founders placed their focus mainly on the digital platforms in order to give their readers a truly interactive experience. We designed the magazine from the ground up with a focus on maintaining a consistent style with an emphasis on providing the audience a clean interactive experience.

Audience Demographics: men and women in their early 20’s to mid 30’s, college educated and located mainly in urban environments, generally unmarried/ no children. Audience Notes: our readers value independent thinking, counter-culture, progressive politics, an appreciation of art and indie-rock, creativity, intelligence as well as witty banter.
